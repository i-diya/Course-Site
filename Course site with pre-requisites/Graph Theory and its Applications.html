Course Content

Fundamentals of graph theory. Topics include: connectivity, planarity, perfect graphs, coloring, matchings and extremal problems.

Basic concepts in Combinatorics. Topics include: counting techniques, inclusion-exclusion principles, permutations, combinations and pigeon-hole principle.
