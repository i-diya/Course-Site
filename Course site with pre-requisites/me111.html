Course Content

Engineering Graphics with mini-drafter: Around half a semester and bit more with following topics to be covered.

  • Introduction to Engineering Graphics

  • Curves

  • Projections of Points

  • Projection of Lines

  • Projection of Planes

  • Projections on Auxiliary Planes

  • Projections of Solids

  • Sections of Solids

  • Intersections of Solids

Engineering Graphics with 2D Drafting Software: 5 weekly computer laboratory sessions covering above using AutoCADÂ® as a drafting software, 5th session on Isometric Projections.
