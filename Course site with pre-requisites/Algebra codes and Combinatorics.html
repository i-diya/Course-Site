Course Content

Syllabus: Algebraic codes: Definition and motivation, parameters, parity check matrix of an algebraic code, basic inequalities, Macwilliams' identity, Perfect codes, Hamming codes, Golay codes, cyclic codes, relation to factorisation of X^{n}-1; MDS codes

Combinatorics: t-designs, Fischers inequality, Finite projective planes, Bruck-Ryser theorem, extensions of Witt designs, ovals in projective planes

Eigen value techniques in graph theory, expander graphs, Ramanujan graphs
