Course Content

The Language of Bits, Assembly Language, Logic Gates, Registers, and Memories, Processor Design, Principles of Pipelining, The Memory System, Multiprocessor Systems, I/O and Storage Devices.

Each concept will be first taught on the basis of the fundamental driving principles. Following this, real world examples (e.g., ARM processors) will be used to emphasize the content.
