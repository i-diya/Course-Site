Course Content

Experiments related to:

  • Modeling of systems: Obtaining transfer function models of mechanical/electrical/electro-mechanical systems Ordinary

  • Performance and stability: Time response, steady-state error, stability etc.

  • Basic modes of feedback control: Proportional, Integral, Derivative.

  • Root locus method of controller design

  • Frequency-domain techniques: Frequency responses

  • Compensator design using frequency response Course projects related to:

  • Advanced control concepts

  • Real life applications of control systems in various fields

  • Applications of Signal Processing Techniques to Control Systems etc.
