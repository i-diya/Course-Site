Course Content

The role of statistics. Graphical and numerical methods for describing and summarising data. Probability. Population distributions. Sampling variability and sampling distributions. Estimation using a single sample. Hypothesis testing a single sample. Comparing two populations or treatments. Simple linear regression and correlation. Case studies.

3. An Introduction to Probability Theory and Its Applications, Vol. 1, William Feller, 3rd edition, Wiley International, 1968.
